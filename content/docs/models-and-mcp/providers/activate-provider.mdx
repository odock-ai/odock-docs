# Activate a provider

Use this workflow when the provider appears in the organisation provider catalog.

<Steps>

<Step>
Open your organisation in Odock.
</Step>

<Step>
Go to **Providers**.
</Step>

<Step>
Search or filter the provider catalog.

</Step>

<Step>
Find the provider you want, such as **OpenAI**, **Anthropic**, **Google**, **Azure OpenAI**, **vLLM**, **Mistral**, or **Custom**.
</Step>

<Step>
Click **Activate**.

</Step>

<Step>
After the provider is active, click **Configure**.
</Step>

</Steps>
