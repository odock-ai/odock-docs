# Create A Team

Use names that match real ownership, such as `Engineering`, `Support`, `Data Platform`, or `Customer Agents`. Avoid creating teams only for temporary access exceptions; use scoped API keys, budgets, or quotas for those cases.

<Steps>

<Step>

Open your organisation workspace.

</Step>

<Step>

Click **Teams** in the sidebar.

</Step>

<Step>

Click **Add New Team**.

</Step>

<Step>

Confirm the organisation field is set to the current organisation.

</Step>

<Step>

Enter the team **Name**.

</Step>

<Step>

Click **Create Team**.

</Step>

<Step>

Confirm the team appears in the teams list.

</Step>

</Steps>
