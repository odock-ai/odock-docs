# Rename A Team

Rename teams when the real ownership group changes. If the team represents a project that ended, also review team keys, budgets, quotas, and usage before deciding whether to keep or delete the team.

<Steps>

<Step>

Open **Teams**.

</Step>

<Step>

Click **Open** on the team row.

</Step>

<Step>

Click the edit action on the team detail page.

</Step>

<Step>

Update the team **Name**.

</Step>

<Step>

Click **Save Changes**.

</Step>

<Step>

Confirm the new name appears on the team detail page and in the teams list.

</Step>

</Steps>
