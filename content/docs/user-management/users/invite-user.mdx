# Invite A User

Use invitation enrollment when the user should activate access themselves and confirm their profile.

<Steps>

<Step>

Open your organisation workspace.

</Step>

<Step>

Click **Users** in the sidebar.

</Step>

<Step>

Open the **Enrollment** tab.

</Step>

<Step>

In **Invitee Email**, enter the user's email address.

</Step>

<Step>

In **Role**, choose **Organisation Admin**, **Manager**, or **User**.

</Step>

<Step>

Click **Send Invitation**.

</Step>

<Step>

Confirm the invitation appears in the **Invitations** table.

</Step>

<Step>

Use **Refresh Invitations** if you need to check whether the invitee has accepted.

</Step>

</Steps>

Choose the initial role carefully. The invitee receives that role when the invitation is completed.
