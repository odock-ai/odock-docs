# Accept An Invitation

Use this tutorial when you received an Odock organisation invitation by email. The invitation must be opened with the same email account that was invited.

## Before You Start

You need:

- The invitation link sent by your organisation.
- Access to the invited email account.
- A GitHub sign-in that matches the invited email.

If the signed-in account does not match the invited email, Odock shows a wrong-account state and asks you to sign out before continuing.

## Accept The Invitation

<Steps>

<Step>

Open the invitation link from the email.

</Step>

<Step>

Review the invitation page before signing in. Check the invited email, organisation, role, status, and expiry.

![Invitation page before login](/images/accept-invitation.png)

</Step>

<Step>

Click the sign-in action and authenticate with the invited email account.

</Step>

<Step>

After login, review the invitation details again and confirm that you are signed in with the invited email.

</Step>

<Step>

Fill the requested profile information.

![Invitation profile form after login](/images/fill-invitation-after-login.png)

</Step>

<Step>

Complete the invitation.

</Step>

<Step>

Confirm that Odock redirects you to the organisation dashboard.

</Step>

</Steps>

After completion, your account becomes active in the organisation with the role assigned by the invitation.

## What Happens Next

The organisation can now review your account in **Users** and add you to teams if needed. Team membership is managed separately from invitation acceptance. See [Add a user to a team](/docs/user-management/users/add-user-to-team) and [Teams](/docs/user-management/teams).

If you need runtime credentials for an application or workflow, those are managed separately through [Virtual API Keys](/docs/management/virtual-api-keys).
