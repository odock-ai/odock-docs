# Usage

The Usage tab is the *what is it costing, and where do tokens go* view.

![Usage tab — tokens and cost over time, tokens vs latency scatter](/gifs/obs-traffic-usage.gif)

## Visualizations

| Chart | Answers |
| --- | --- |
| **Tokens and Cost** | *Is the bill explainable?* Daily token total and cost in USD on a dual axis. |
| **Tokens vs Latency** | *Is the bill costing user time?* Scatter where each dot is one AI request, with tokens on x and latency on y. |

Tokens and Cost moving together means volume is driving spend. Cost rising with flat tokens points at a model swap or a pricing change — see [Model pricing](/docs/models-and-mcp/models/model-pricing).

## Workflow

<Steps>

<Step>
Open this tab when the **Tokens and Cost** KPI card on the header shows growth.
</Step>

<Step>
Confirm the two series rise together. If they diverge, switch the Usage Records breakdown to **Model** to find which model is responsible.
</Step>

<Step>
Look at **Tokens vs Latency** for a new high-token, high-latency cluster — that is usually a context-window change in an application.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Tips

<Callout type="tip">
The scatter only includes AI records. MCP usage is measured in bytes — open the [MCP tab](/docs/observability/traffic-analytics/explore-mcp) for that.
</Callout>
